About Me

    Early Life

SpotYourShow

Web Application

This is a web application that helps users find concerts! You begin by authorizing access to your Spotify account. From there you are taken to our application page and will see a list of all the artists you follow. You can select artists whose shows you would like to go to and ultimately purchase tickets through the Ticketmaster site.
